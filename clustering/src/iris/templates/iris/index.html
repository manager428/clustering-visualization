Some Intuition

Before we begin, let's draw a basic example. Imagine two groups of people, differing in appearance. One is tall, while the other is short. The taller group clearly weighs more than the shorter group. The features of these two groups of people are "height" and "weight." If we were to graph each person's height and weight on a scatter plot, with the x-axis as "weight", and the y-axis as "height." The two groups are clearly separate, distinctly showing which group each person belongs to.
